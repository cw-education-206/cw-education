Creating Visual Literacy in Educational Spaces

This video is a compilation of narratives of significantly visual learners and explores how they utilise visual mediums to learn and process information. The interviewees are associated with various visual techniques such as fine art, sketch noting, film-making, and tattoos; through these techniques, they learn what is taught in formal educational spaces and utilise them as visual mediums to be heard and create a significant impact. This video is a public pedagogy document of my critical writing paper on ‘Can We See and Imagine What We Learn?: Exploring the Role of Visual Elements and Narrative Imagination in Education, exploring the role of visual elements and imagination in learning.
