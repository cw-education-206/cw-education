Memories of Partition

My project is a website called ‘memories of partition: an art and literature library’. The idea is to create a repository of partition related resources or recommendations including books (both fiction and non-fiction), poetry, podcasts, films and also some artwork that depicts the partition in a light different than the mainstream narratives. Memories of Partition will be a place which offers content in various forms and languages for everyone, irrespective of how one learns. It has have something to read, to watch, to just observe and something to listen to. With different resources available, this page is just an example of how learning about history can be diversified by introducing different forms of content, more focused on the oral histories and personal narratives so that one can think about history through the lens of ‘counter-memory’.
