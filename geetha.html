Geetha Thatikonda

Evolve Through Art

Art is one of the first forms of visual expression and language in children. It is a beautiful medium that allows one to express their emotional content. Art education has the potential to foster creativity and imagination in students which encourages children to form logical and innovative ideas. In my experience working as an elementary teacher, I was able to leverage art education to facilitate an engaging classroom environment. Students started to participate more in class and share their insights. Additionally, they started to collaborate with one another and build upon their teamwork skills. I was able to witness the impact created on students’ socio-emotional development with the way they would empathize with one another and encourage their peers. I believe that artists can bring out something that cannot be seen or felt easily and can start a dialogue with the viewer. An art piece can generate multiple meanings and help one make conceptual connections. I aim to showcase this through my public pedagogy project and also propose interactive and playful methods of teaching that can act as an alternative to the static instructional practices.
