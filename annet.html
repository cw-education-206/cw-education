The Dialogue Series

Dialogue is a crucial determinant in a student-teacher interaction. But, in the Indian educational system meaningful interactions between teachers and students are not seen frequently. Moreover the onus to build healthy relations is mainly assumed to be for the teachers. When the majority of the discussions around dialogue focus on students, there exists very limited spaces for teachers to open up. Even in academic writing, everything found related to the topic is mainly what a teacher should do and not what students should. So, my public pedagogy initiative revolves around initiating dialogues with students and teachers. As part of it, I have started doing a series called The Dialogue Series, which will have various episodes regarding dialogue. The first episode was the students' perspective video. The purpose of this episode is to make students aware of the need for spaces and opportunities for teachers to freely open up about the difficulties that they face while teaching.
