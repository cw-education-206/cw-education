Fluid Borders

Fluid borders was an attempt to make the people around engage with different cultural backgrounds by introducing them to multicultural literature. Since this introduction is basically to promote cultural awareness in the current university setup and for the people around who come from different geographical backgrounds, the main focus was to do this through immigrant centric stories that talk about movement and struggles associated.

To engage, communicate our existing understanding, broadening them with the help of these stories and to further challenge the notion of solid boundaries that restrict the movement of different cultures towards each other was the main objective of this project called - Fluid Borders.
