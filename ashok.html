Blame It On School

Education as we know it today only came into being a few centuries ago with the advent of industrialization. This modern education lies on the foundation of Human capital theory and is often positioned as the panacea to all modern anomalies in human society, but nothing could be far from the truth. As the modern system is operationalized with a reductivist investment attitude, creating labor for the workforce rather than enlightened individuals for the society and contexts they live in. Education is not engaging with the individual at levels of the self, the society, ecology, or the unique mind; but rather pursued only for economic pursuits. Thereby, affecting the overall quality and effectiveness that education/knowledge has to offer to mankind. It is fair to say that edUcatIon missing the U and I leading a mass production of literate people educated people but without the essence of inquiry or wisdom, or even any changes to social challenges (discrimination based on sex, caste) which have still not uprooted despite the growing levels of education. I have made a series of manual artworks, 'Arti'culating the dilemmas, shortcomings, and paradoxes of the modern education system.
