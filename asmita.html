Disability and Education

The life of a student with a disability is not an easy one. The simplest of tasks that we often take for granted or don’t even pay attention to also take a lot of effort for them. Additionally, students with disabilities not only face challenges at an infrastructural level with the absence of lifts, ramps, braille, learning support etc. but also at a social level as they have to navigate an insensitive and unaware society that compounds their challenges. This video is an effort to document some of the challenges faced by students with disabilities and then use it to sensitize the masses about them. The aim is to spread awareness and promote the values of inclusion and support for students with disabilities.
